    Frequently Asked Questions

    Have questions about our floral products, services, or delivery options? Here, we answer some of the most common inquiries from our customers to help you find the information you need. If you can't find your answer here, feel free to reach out to us directly.

    What types of flowers do you offer?

    We offer a wide variety of flowers, including roses, lilies, tulips, orchids, and seasonal flowers. Our collection is curated to provide options for every occasion and preference.

    How do I care for my flowers?

    Each type of flower has specific care instructions. Generally, keep the flowers in a cool environment, trim the stems every few days, change the water regularly, and avoid placing them in direct sunlight.

    Do you offer same-day delivery?

    Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 12 PM local time. For more details on our delivery options and areas, please visit our Delivery Information page.

    Can I customize my bouquet?

    Absolutely! You can customize your bouquet by choosing your preferred flowers, colors, and arrangement style. Contact our customer service team to discuss your preferences and place a custom order.

    What is your return policy?

    If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We will work with you to resolve the issue, which may include a replacement or a refund, depending on the circumstances.
